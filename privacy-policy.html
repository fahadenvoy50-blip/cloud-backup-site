1. Information We Collect

Cloud Backup & Data Recovery accesses the following data solely to provide backup and restore functionality:

  • Contacts
  • SMS messages
  • Call logs
  • Google Drive (only files created by this app)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is backed up directly to your personal Google Drive. We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to any external servers.

3. Google Drive Access

We use Google Drive API with drive.file scope. We can only access files that our app creates. We cannot access any other files in your Google Drive.

4. Data Retention

Your backup files remain in your Google Drive. You can delete them anytime. Uninstalling the app does not delete your Drive backups.

5. Third Party Services

This app uses Google Sign-In and Google Drive API. Google's privacy policy applies.
